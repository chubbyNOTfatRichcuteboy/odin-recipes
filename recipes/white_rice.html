White Rice

This fluffy white rice is the perfect base for any entree. Combine it with any dish--chicken, beef--even frogs, and it'll still be delectable!

This recipe involves a rice cooker, rice, and a sink

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups white rice
  • 2 cups water

Steps:

  1. Wash rice 3 times
  2. Put equal parts water to rice
  3. Place into rice cooker and let it cook
  4. Enjoy!
