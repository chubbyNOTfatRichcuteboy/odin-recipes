Cheesecake Brownies

These cheesecake brownies combine some ooey, gooey brownies together with rich, flavorful cheesecake to form a decadent delight

The recipe consists of two portions: making and baking the brownie batter, as well as whipping up fresh cheesecake to layer on top of the brownies

Ingredients:

Chocolate batter

  • 16 tablespoons unsalted butter, room temp
  • 2 and a quarter cups granulated sugar
  • 1 and a quarter cups cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon table salt
  • 1 tablespooon pure vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Cream cheese batter

  • 16 ounces cream cheese, roomp temp
  • 3 quarters cup granulated sugar
  • half cup unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • quarter cup heavy cream
  • 2 large eggs

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F. Line a 9" x 13" pan with a parchment paper sling then lightly grease the parchment and exposed sides of the pan.
  2. To make the brownie batter: In a large saucepan, melt the butter, then stir in the sugar, cooking until the mixture is smooth and shiny.
  3. Remove from the heat and stir in the cocoa, salt, baking powder, espresso powder, and vanilla. Allow to cool slightly, around 5 minutes.
  4. Whisk in the eggs, then the flour, stirring until smooth.
  5. To make the cream cheese batter: Beat the cream cheese until no lumps remain. Mix in the sugar and flour, then the vanilla, cream, and eggs.
  6. Fold the chocolate chips into the brownie batter.
  7. To assemble the cheesecake brownies: Spoon half the brownie batter into the prepared pan, then top with the cream cheese batter.
  8. Dollop the remaining brownie batter onto the cream cheese, swirling the two together.
  9. Bake the cheesecake brownies for 40 to 45 minutes, until a tester inserted into the center comes out clean, and the edges are set.
  10. Remove the cheesecake brownies from the oven, and loosen the edges with a table knife. Cool completely (preferably overnight) before cutting.
  11. Enjoy!
